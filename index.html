Ying Ma


Hi there! 👋 I’m a PhD Candidate in the Human-Computer Interaction Group at The University of Melbourne, under the supervision of Assoc. Prof. Jorge Goncalves, Dr. Jarrod Knibbe and Dr. Zhanna Sarsenbayeva.

My research encompasses the domains of human-computer interaction, social computing, LLM and geo-location informatics. Especially, my research focuses on online communities such as social networking sites, including the perception and behavior of individuals regarding their online privacy, strategies to combat misinformation and facilitate informed credibility decisions, designing novel interventions & interfaces to enhance user experiences in online environments, etc.

Feel free to contact me in case you have interest or questions regarding my work. 🤝
